Isohedral Tilings & Folding Tiles

Introduction

This project focuses on expanding the study of isohedral tilings by developing algorithms to identify and transform specific classes of tiles.

We will focus on two phases:

  1. Detecting Isohedral Tiles with Rotational Symmetries
  2. Folding the Tile into a Doubly-Covered Triangle
